About Tsquare.o

Bringing Nigeria to Vienna, one meal and hairstyle at a time

Welcome to Tsquare.o, your number one destination for authentic Nigerian cuisine and premium hair extensions in Vienna, Austria. Founded with a passion for sharing our rich culture, we've been serving the community since 2020.

Our food is prepared with love, using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients to bring you the true taste of Nigeria. From our party jollof rice to our rich egusi soup, every dish tells a story of home.

In our hair collection, we offer only the highest quality human hair wigs and extensions. Whether you're looking for a lace front wig, braid wigs, or virgin hair bundles, we've got you covered with products that make you feel confident and beautiful.
